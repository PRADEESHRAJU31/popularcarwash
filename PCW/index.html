Our Story

More Than a Car Wash

Popular Car Wash started in 2023 as a single bay operation run by founder HariHaran, a man with a passion for pristine vehicles and honest service and have a 15 years of experience in automobile industry. He worked in leading car manufacture in Sales and Services sector, it begins with one pressure washer and a dream grew into the region's most trusted auto care destination — built entirely on word of mouth, repeat customers, and an unwavering belief that every car deserves to be treated like it's brand new. We will provide a refreshing experience to all customers in the term of car washing and car detailing, in spite of providing an outstanding service to all.

🤝
Family-Run, Community-First

We are locally owned and deeply connected to our community. Every rupee you spend here stays local. We know our regulars by name — and by car.

🏆
15 Years of Industry Experience

HariHaran brings over 15 years of expertise from leading automobile manufacturers in sales and service — every vehicle gets the expert care it deserves.

🔁
Why Customers Keep Returning

Our 98% satisfaction rate is a reflection of trust built over thousands of washes. Consistent quality, transparent pricing, and a team that truly cares.
